About the Company

Angelucci Fine Jewelry began in Italy by Domenico Angelucci in June of 1907 in the picturesque hillside town of Atina, nestled in the Comino Valley in between Roma and Napoli. One of the oldest cities in Italia, Atina is an ancient city that has as part of its rich history a long line of metalsmiths and swordmakers. My ancestors are part of that story. It is my destiny to fulfill that story with you as the authors and each beautiful piece we create together are the lines of the pages, the chapters of the memories that build the legacies and the heirlooms of our families. It is my honor to be able to take part and celebrate with you the most important moments in your lives! Since 1907, the Angelucci family has provided only the finest handcrafted Italian jewelry to your family with the idea of always listening to the customer and providing the best service making memories that last generations.

 

About Pasquale

Born in Los Angeles in 1974, Pasquale Angelucci studied marketing, international business and gemmology making him an expert in the jewelry sector. At 17, he became manager of his parents' Los Angeles store and went on to complete a degree in Translation and Interpretation in order to expand his language skills to clearly communicate with his clients and suppliers. He then went on to work for two years(2004-2006) with some of the finest jewelry designers in Valenza, Italy becoming a diamond buyer for a giant Indian wholesaler of diamonds. Living and working in Italy left an indelible mark on Pasquale's perspective and understanding of the business. Hence he acquired valuable insight into the international jewelry market on every level. Working one on one with his clients in the privacy of his beautiful corner office, Pasquale is able to listen and focus on the individuals' needs and desires. A unique collaboration is born every time Pasquale works with his clients on the jewelry of their dreams. The customer becomes an integral part of the design process and so their piece is truly theirs and no one else's. This is the process, this is the heart of Angelucci Jewelry custom jewelry design...
