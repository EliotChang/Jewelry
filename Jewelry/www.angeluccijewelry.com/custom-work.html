Custom Work

History

Our Jewelry is made by master artisans who are descendants of the ironsmiths of the ancient Romans. It is meticulously forged with the utmost care and attention to detail, quality and the most classic, fundamental ideals for design. Every ring, every amulet, every pendant, every piece is made especially for that particular client and no one else. This is what makes Angelucci Jewelry different: personalized, high end, custom jewelry that reflects a clear expression of beauty inspired by the true luxury of Nature.

   Engagement Rings

Choosing the right engagement ring can feel like pulling teeth sometimes. At Angelucci Fine Jewelry, we soothe our guests’ anxiety by custom tailoring every engagement ring to fit your exact specifications. Angelucci Fine Jewelry prides itself on providing only the best quality Italian jewelry. Our streamlined 3d printing rendering to wax to casting to finished product process is a unique collaboration between designer and guest.
