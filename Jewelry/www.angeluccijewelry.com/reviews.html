Reviews

Rick and Yana Gil De Montes- Client and Great Friend since 1988

In November of 2010 I made the choice to marry my best friend. So, of course, I needed an engagement ring. But not just any engagement ring... I needed something special, unique and personal. If you're a typical guy - which I am - you understand that choosing a ring for your (hopefully) soon-to-be fiancé is a daunting and damn near impossible task. I needed to get this right. And, I was clueless.

I called Pasquale with Angelucci Jewelry and explained what I wanted to create. Later that week, he drove from La Habra to Glendale late one evening to explain to me the 4 C's, review a handful of diamonds he hand delivered AND to have a conversation with me about the setting, the design of the ring and the process.

The entire process took about six weeks and I couldn't be happier with the result. Throughout the process, he was extremely patient with me while I tried to explain my picky ideas about the look, feel and design of the ring. His response was to provide 3D renderings of the ring (wireframe and lifelike) to help me feel both at ease and confident about my choice. Then, he provided a plastic mold then later a copper mold so I could actually see and feel what the ring would look like.

My wife was astonished by the ring and she couldn't be happier. She absolutely loved her ring! I really felt like Pashko was my partner in making it such a special piece of jewelry. I have a feeling this ring will be in our family for generations to come. All heirlooms have their beginning.

Pashko really made me feel like I was an important client while at the same time educating me about the process and helping me to understand what I could expect. He was emphatic about what a special time in my life this was and I felt like he really got where I was coming from. In the end, I couldn't be happier with the quality, the price or the treatment I got from Angelucci Jewelry. They really do make you feel like you're part of a family.

Rick and Yana Gil De Montes- Client and Great Friend since 1988
