about us

Angelucci Fine Jewelry began in Italy by Domenico Angelucci in June of 1907 in the picturesque hillside town of Atina, nestled in the Comino Valley in between Roma and Napoli. One of the oldest cities in Italia, Atina is an ancient city that has as part of its rich history a long line of metalsmiths and swordmakers.Â My ancestors are part of that story.
